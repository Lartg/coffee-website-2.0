Cost
Cheaper

A 12oz latte costs -on average- $4.00.   However, ~$17 nets a 1 lbs bag of whole bean, speciality coffee.   That comes out to ~23 cups of coffee/bag, or ~$0.75/cup.   It's even cheaper when buying commercial coffee in bulk.   Coffee is a commodity.   It's cheap.   Coffee being expensive at a cafe is a result of the accrued costs of running a business.

Time
Faster

Its out of one's way to get coffee from a cafe.  Factor in ~5 minutes in line, and ~5 minutes until one gets their drink, and they've at least 10 minutes getting coffee.   At home one can brew coffee in less than 5 minutes, saving time and money.

Flavor
Brew perfect coffee

Something about the routine of brewing is satsfying and calming.   But beyond that, one is able -with experience- to control variables of brewing to curtail their recipe to perfectly match their tastes.
